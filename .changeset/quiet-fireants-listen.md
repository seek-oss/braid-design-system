**vars:** Add new backgrounds and light variant border colors

**New backgrounds**
The following backrounds are now available on the `vars.backgroundColor` theme object:
- `neutralActive`
- `neutralHover`
- `neutralSoftActive`
- `neutralSoftHover`

**New borderColors**
The following border colors are now available on the `vars.borderColor` theme object:
- `brandAccentLight`
- `criticalLight`
- `formAccentLight`
