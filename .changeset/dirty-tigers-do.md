**Inline:** Add *noWrap* prop to support single-row compositions


**EXAMPLE USAGE:**
```jsx
  <Inline space="small" noWrap>
    <Placeholder width={20} height={48} />
    <Placeholder width={80} height={48} />
    <Placeholder width={40} height={48} />
  </Inline>
```
